  • Donating blood can save a life, and it only takes about an hour of your time.
  • The demand for blood in India is increasing by 10-15% every year due to population growth, medical advancements, and lifestyle diseases.
  • Approximately 12 million units of blood are required annually in India, but only 9 million units are available.
  • One unit of blood can save up to three lives in India, as the blood components can be separated and used for different patients.
  • The Indian Red Cross Society (IRCS) is the largest blood bank in India, with over 150 blood banks and 1000 blood collection centers.
  • The eligibility criteria for blood donation in India are similar to those in the US, with some variations based on local regulations and guidelines.

Why Resurrection?

As we stand on the precipice of saving lives, Resurrection calls upon individuals to join us in our noble mission of donating blood.
Since our inception in 2022, Resurrection has been a beacon of hope, consistently striving to bridge the gap between life and death through the selfless act of blood donation.

Our track record speaks volumes. Over the past years, Resurrection has facilitated countless successful blood donation drives, rallying communities and individuals alike to contribute to the greater good.
Through our relentless efforts, we've managed to amass a vast network of donors, ensuring a steady supply of blood for those in need.

But our achievements extend beyond mere numbers. Resurrection has become synonymous with reliability and efficiency in blood collection and distribution.
Our streamlined processes guarantee that every drop of blood donated reaches its intended recipient promptly and safely, making a tangible difference in the lives of patients battling illnesses or injuries.

Moreover, Resurrection prioritizes transparency and accountability in all our endeavors.
Donors can rest assured that their contributions are handled with the utmost care and integrity, with full transparency regarding where their blood donations are allocated.

Yet, our work is far from over.The need for blood remains constant, and every donation, no matter how small, holds the power to save a life. By donating blood through Resurrection,
you become a vital part of our lifesaving mission, offering hope and comfort to those facing medical emergencies.

Join us in our journey of compassion and solidarity. Together, let's continue to write the story of resilience and humanity, one drop of blood at a time.
